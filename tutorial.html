Magicicada tutorial

Hi! Welcome to the tutorial. We will show you how you can help us to identify Magicicada species!.

The application is really simple. It loads a photo from aProject Noah Mission and asks you this question: Is it a magicicada?

You will have 3 possible answers:

  • Yes,
  • No, and
  • I don't know

All you have to do is to click in one of the three possible answers and you will be moved to the next step.

The final step will show three types of magicicadas:

  • Septendecim

    by Fontaine K, Cooley J, Simon C

  • Cassini

    by Fontaine K, Cooley J, Simon C

  • Septendecula

    by Fonatine K, Cooley J, Simon C

Select in the drop down menu the species that you think is the right one and click in the save button. Your answer will be saved and a new task will be loaded for you.
